Aftercare

There are, a couple general rules of thumb to keep in mind when nursing your tattoo. This is especially true for the first week or so when most problems arise. However, they are rare in general, good hygiene truly is a modern miracle. Follow some basic instructions, keep a good routine and it should come out nicely. I have also put together a pdf that goes over everything you need to know, It also covers medical protective film, or "snakeskin".

Initial Care and First 2 Weeks

The first week is the most crucial in terms of inflammation prevention and healing outcome in the long run. It's not rocket science , but in order to heal properly, you need to mind a few things.

  • Plastic wrap can stay on 3-4 hours after being applied. See PDF for instructions on medical protective wrap, or "snakeskin"
  • Dont go swimming, and dont shower for too long, since it softens the skin.
  • Dont cook it, ideally you dont expose it to the sun at all for the first 2 weeks. But most people dont sleep in coffins, so be responsible, okay?
